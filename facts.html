FACTS ABOUT BIRDS


At one point, scientist estimated 200 to 400 billion individual birds have coexisted at one point within the last decade
By the year 2100, up to 14% of all bird species may become extinct. And 7-25% (28-56% on oceanic islands) may become "functionally extinct."
Since the 1500s, birds have been declining both in terms of species numbers (by about 500 species) and numbers of individuals (by about 20-25%)
A bald eagle's white head may make it look bald. But actually the name comes from an old English word, "balde," meaning white
